# Windows 10 Cheat Sheet

#### Launch Emoji Keyboard
`Win+.` or `Win+;`

![Emoji Keyboard](/images/win10-emoji.png)

#### Sleep Windows 10 with a Keyboard Shortcut
`Win+X` followed by `U` then `S`

![Win+X](/images/WinKeyPlusX.png)
